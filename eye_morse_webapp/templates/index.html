📖 Blink Pattern Guide

Hold your eyes closed for the specified duration:

● DOT (.) - Short Blink
Duration: 0.1 - 0.4 seconds
Quick, natural blink
━ DASH (-) - Medium Blink
Duration: 0.4 - 0.9 seconds
Hold eyes closed slightly longer
⌫ Morse Backspace - Delete Last Dot/Dash
Duration: 0.9 - 1.5 seconds
Removes last symbol from current letter
⌫⌫ Letter Backspace - Delete Last Letter
Duration: 1.5 - 2.0 seconds
Removes last decoded letter
␣ SPACE - End Word
Duration: 2.5 - 3.0 seconds OR wait 2 seconds
Adds space after current letter
↵ ENTER - Finalize & Speak
Duration: 2.0 - 2.5 seconds
Finalizes text and speaks it aloud
💡 Tip:

Letters auto-decode after 2 seconds of no blinking. For better accuracy, train the model using python recorder.py
